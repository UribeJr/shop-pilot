# Notion MCP Challenge — Demo Script

Use this to showcase **what Notion MCP enables** in your submission.

---

## The one-line pitch

> **Shop Pilot syncs your Shopify store into Notion. Your AI (via Notion MCP) can then search, read, and reason over that data — giving you store intelligence as a conversational superpower.**

---

## Why MCP matters here

| Without Notion MCP | With Notion MCP |
|--------------------|-----------------|
| AI doesn't know your store | AI reads your reports from Notion |
| You copy-paste data or use dashboards | You ask natural language questions |
| Dashboards are static | Your AI reasons over live Notion content |

**Shop Pilot bridges Shopify → Notion. Notion MCP bridges Notion → your AI.**

---

## 3-minute demo flow

### Part 1: Show the write path (30 sec)
1. Open Shop Pilot → **Generate report now**
2. Open your Notion page — show **Store Dashboard**, **Weekly Reports**, **Alerts**, **Products to Watch**
3. Say: *"Shop Pilot wrote this from my Shopify store."*

### Part 2: Show the MCP read path (90 sec)
1. In Cursor (or ChatGPT/Claude with Notion MCP connected):
   - **"What are my top selling products this week?"**
   - *AI searches Notion, finds Products to Watch, answers*
   - **"What needs restocking?"**
   - *AI reads Alerts and inventory data from Notion*
   - **"Summarize my store performance"**
   - *AI reads KPI History and Weekly Reports*

2. Point out: *"The AI has no custom API. It's using Notion MCP to read the pages Shop Pilot created."*

### Part 3: Show Notion as the control surface (60 sec)
1. Edit **Merchant Preferences** in Notion: e.g. *"Business focus: prioritize repeat customers"*
2. Run sync again
3. Show how the report changed (tone, recommendations, alert focus)
4. Say: *"Notion isn't just storage — it's where I set intent. The app reads that and tailors the output."*

## Prompts that highlight MCP

Use these in your AI (with Notion MCP connected):

- *"Search my Notion for the latest weekly store report and tell me the key takeaways."*
- *"What products are in my Products to Watch database?"*
- *"From my Alerts in Notion, what needs my attention?"*
- *"Compare my 7-day vs 30-day KPIs from the KPI History."*

The AI will invoke Notion MCP tools to search and read — that's the superpower.
